About the Museum


The IFSU Heritage Museum serves as a bridge between the ancient traditions of the Ifugao people and the modern generation. It is a sanctuary for Ifugao wisdom, showcasing the ingenuity of our ancestors in agriculture, weaving, and woodcarving.


OUR MISSION:

"To preserve, protect, and promote the cultural heritage of the Ifugao people through education and exhibition of sacred artifacts."
